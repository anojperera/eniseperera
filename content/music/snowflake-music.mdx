This piece was written for a little musical I was creating at the time.

## My Journey

**Thought**  
I imagined delicate snowflakes falling slowly and sparkling in the light.

**Rhythm**  
Gentle and magical — almost like tiptoeing across fresh snow.

**Action**  
I played a soft, twinkly melody that I could picture the snowflakes dancing to.
