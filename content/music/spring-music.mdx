I wrote this little piece to celebrate the arrival of beautiful Spring.

## My Journey

**Thought**  
I looked out the window and saw a butterfly inspecting the flowers that were just starting to bloom. Everything felt fresh and full of promise.

**Rhythm**  
The butterfly moved so quickly from flower to flower, almost like it was dancing. I imagined light, bouncy notes that flutter up and down.

**Action**  
I sat at the piano and started playing a simple tune that matched the excited energy I saw. This is what came out!
