A piece inspired by the beautiful falling leaves and the golden colours of Autumn.

## My Journey

**Thought**  
I watched the leaves gently floating down outside. They looked calm but a little sad, like they were saying goodbye to summer.

**Rhythm**  
The slow drifting motion made me think of a waltz — graceful and a little wistful.

**Action**  
I wrote a tune that feels like walking through crunchy leaves while the wind plays around you.
