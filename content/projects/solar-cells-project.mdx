This was a wonderful school project that I completed about two weeks ago, exploring the fascinating world of solar energy. I learned all about photovoltaic cells and how they magically convert sunlight into electricity!

## The Poster

![Solar Cells Poster](https://eniseperera-media.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/jun-2026/solar_cells.png)

Solar cells are like tiny power plants that capture the sun's rays and turn them into clean, renewable energy. In my project, I illustrated how these cells work, from the basic science of photons exciting electrons to real-world applications like solar panels on rooftops and in space!

It was so inspiring to discover how solar power can help protect our planet by reducing our reliance on fossil fuels. I even included fun facts about the efficiency of different types of solar cells and how they're becoming more affordable every day.

This project made me excited about a future powered by the sun – it's such a bright idea! 🌞
