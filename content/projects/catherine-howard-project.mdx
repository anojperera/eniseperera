This was a school project I completed in March 2026 with two of my school friends. We worked together on a poster about Catherine Howard!

## The Project

![Catherine Howard Poster](https://eniseperera-media.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/mar-2026/catherine_howard.gif)

Catherine Howard was the fifth wife of King Henry VIII. Our project explored her life, the Tudor court, and the dramatic events surrounding her.

It was great collaborating with my friends on the research and the animated elements in the GIF!
