This is a lab safety project I created. All the images were sketched on an iPad and then combined into the final poster design.

## The Poster

![Lab Safety Poster](https://eniseperera-media.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/oct-2025/lab_safety.png)

The poster covers key safety guidelines to follow when working in a science laboratory, including proper equipment use, handling of chemicals, and emergency procedures.

I drew all the icons and illustrations digitally on my iPad before assembling everything into this informative poster.
