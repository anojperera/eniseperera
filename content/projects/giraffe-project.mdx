This was a school project I completed on the 24th of March 2026. It's all about giraffes and their amazing features!

## The Poster

![Giraffe Poster](https://eniseperera-media.s3.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/mar-2026/giraffe.png)

Giraffes are the tallest land animals on Earth. They have incredibly long necks that help them reach leaves high up in the trees of the African savanna. 

In this project, I included lots of interesting facts about their habitat, diet (mostly acacia leaves!), unique spotted patterns, and why they are such gentle giants. I had a lot of fun researching and creating the illustrations for this poster.

I learned that giraffes can run really fast and have powerful kicks to defend themselves. Their long tongues are also blueish-purple to protect them from sunburn!

This project helped me understand more about wildlife conservation and the importance of protecting animals like giraffes in their natural environment.
