Kent Junior League Round 1 at Tunbridge Wells Sports Centre where I took part in 3 relays, 4 x 50 Freestyle, 4 x 50 medley and 8 x 50 freestyle. I also took part in 2 individual races, 50m front crawl and 50m breaststroke. I won both of them and even better Maidstone Club finished 1st. I am in lane 3.
